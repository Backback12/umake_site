FAQ

Who can apply?

Any student currently enrolled in a post-secondary institution. We welcome beginners and veterans alike!

What if I don't have hardware?

No problem! We have a dedicated hardware lab where you can sign out everything from Arduinos to 3D printers.

Is it free?

Yes! Admission, hardware rentals, food, and swag are all completely free for accepted hackers.
